# Self Assessment

Throughout this course, I developed a much deeper understanding of Artificial Intelligence and its applications. At the beginning of the semester, my knowledge of AI was primarily limited to general concepts and popular applications. In my previous internship, I had the opportunity to implement AI solutions but felt limited by my own knowledge of AI. By the end of the course, I gained experience with intelligent agents, search algorithms, adversarial AI, constraint satisfaction problems, Large Language Models, and the ethical considerations surrounding Artificial Intelligence.

One of the areas where I experienced the most growth was in understanding how AI systems make decisions and solve problems. Through programming assignments and class activities, I learned how different AI techniques can be applied to a variety of scenarios and how implementation choices can significantly affect performance and outcomes. Working on projects involving search algorithms, game-playing agents, and constraint satisfaction problems helped me connect theoretical concepts with practical applications.

One of the most valuable skills I developed throughout the semester was the ability to research and evaluate information from multiple sources. Because Artificial Intelligence evolves so rapidly, staying informed about new developments and emerging technologies is essential. The literature reviews and research assignments helped me strengthen my ability to analyze information critically and understand how current research influences the development of modern AI systems. I also gained a greater appreciation for the broader impact of Artificial Intelligence. Through essays, debates, and literature reviews, I explored topics such as ethics, social impact, and human intelligence. These activities showed me that Artificial Intelligence is not only a technical discipline but also one that requires thoughtful consideration of its effects on people and society. They also showed me how to listen to other opinions and debate with mutual respect. Collaborating with my team was an enjoyable experience. While there were some obstacles along the way, we managed to complete the course with several successful projects that I am proud of.

The topics that interested me most were Large Language Models, intelligent agents, and real-world AI applications. These areas demonstrated how AI concepts learned in the classroom can be applied to solve practical problems and create systems capable of assisting people in meaningful ways. They also motivated me to continue learning about Artificial Intelligence beyond the scope of this course.

I am motivated to start applying what I have learned and experimenting with my own solutions using the knowledge I have gained throughout the semester. One of the projects I would like to build is my own version of Jarvis! I have already explored different libraries and AI models, but I lacked the time to pursue the idea further because of other responsibilities. In any case, this course has opened new paths for me to continue exploring, building, and learning.

I am very satisfied with what I accomplished during this semester. The course strengthened both my technical knowledge and critical thinking skills while providing a strong foundation in Artificial Intelligence concepts. As a Computer Engineering student, I believe the knowledge and experience gained throughout this course will be valuable in my future academic and professional career. More importantly, the course encouraged me to remain curious, continue learning, and stay informed in a field that continues to evolve at a rapid pace.
