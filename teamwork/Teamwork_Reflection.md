During this course, I had the opportunity to work with classmates during presentations, discussions, and debates. Even though many assignments focused on individual understanding, collaborating with others helped me better understand some of the concepts covered in class.

One thing I noticed is that everyone approaches problems differently. Some of us looked at problems from a more technical point of view, while others focused more on the theoretical aspects of finding a solution. Additionally, I appreciated how we were able to help each other in areas where we sometimes got stuck. For example, in some programming assignments there could be multiple solutions to the same problem, and by working together we were able to discuss different approaches and apply the one that best fit the situation.

I also enjoyed working with others during presentations and debates because they encouraged discussion instead of simply looking up information and writing a paragraph about it. These activities promoted critical thinking and required us to listen to different opinions and perspectives, which are key aspects of a professional working environment.

A major milestone was our oral demonstration, where we successfully presented our work and were able to explain each component clearly and confidently. This achievement reflected the effort we put in as a team and demonstrated the hard work required to understand each concept individually before presenting it to others. It also showed the importance of preparation, communication, and teamwork in successfully delivering a technical presentation.

In conclusion, teamwork contributed positively to my learning experience throughout this course. It helped me strengthen my communication skills, gain new perspective , and develop a deeper understanding of the topics discussed in class.
