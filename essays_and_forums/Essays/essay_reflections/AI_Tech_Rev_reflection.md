# AI Technology Timeline

## Objective

The objective of this assignment was to examine the evolution of Artificial Intelligence and identify important milestones that contributed to the development of modern AI systems.

## Summary

This assignment explored the progression of Artificial Intelligence over the years, highlighting key developments such as virtual assistants, machine learning applications, image generation models, and large-scale AI systems. The discussion focused on how AI has evolved from relatively simple applications into technologies capable of generating content, solving complex problems, and creating interactive environments.

The assignment also explored recent advancements in AI and considered some of the challenges that accompany continued technological growth, including computational requirements and environmental concerns.

## Final Thoughts

This assignment helped me better understand how quickly Artificial Intelligence has evolved over the past few decades. Looking at the timeline allowed me to see how many technologies that are common today were built upon years of research and development.

Although this was not my strongest assignment, it still helped me appreciate the pace at which AI continues to advance and the impact it has across different industries. It also made me think about the future of AI and some of the challenges that may arise as these technologies continue to become more powerful and widely adopted.
