# AI and Society: Ethical Impact Analysis 

## Objective 

The objective of this essay was to analyze the societal impacts of Artificial Intelligence Through the application  of three ethical frameworks: Consequentialism, deontological ethics, and virtue ethics. The project examined both the benefits and risks associated with Ai and evaluated how ethical principles can be applied to guide responsible AI development and deployment. 

## Summary

Our group analyzed four major areas where Artificial Intelligence has a significant impact on society: environmnetal sustainability, deepfakes and misinformation, positive applications in healthcaare, education, and research, and the effects of AI on employment and labor markets. Each topic was evaluated using the ethical framework to determine the effects of AI in society and individuals. 

My contribution focused on the environmental impact of Artificial Intelligence. I research the energy consumption, carbon emissions, and resource usage associated with large AI models and data centers. Through this analysis, I argued wheter the societal benefits of AI justify its environmental costs and discussed the ethical responsibiliteis that companies and organizations have in minimizing environmental harm.
